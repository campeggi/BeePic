Geolocate photos by time (ver. 0.1 - experimental)


Aim: geo-locate photos according to shooting time

Requirements:
- EXIF data with shooting time in the image files
- a layer of reference points with date-time attributes (GPS track-points)

Output: a layer of points showing the position each photo was taken

Description:
This plugin locate digital photos according to shooting time thanks to the comparison with a layer of reference points (usually the points of a GPS track). Image files must have EXIF data with the shooting time ('DateTimeOriginal' tag) and the reference layer must have a field with date and time of acquisition in table of attributes.
The plugin reads the shooting time of each photo and looks for the reference point with the same time or the two points whose date-time contain it; in this last case the plugin calculates a proportion between times and positions to locate the photo on the right distance in the segment between the two points.
Often the clock of the digital camera isn't synchronized with GPS time; to correct the consequent error you need to insert the delay of the photos over the reference points. If this value is unknown you can select a photo taken in a known place, show its position on the map and change the delay value until the right position is reached.
